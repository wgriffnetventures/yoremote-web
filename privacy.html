Yo Remote Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

Yo Remote is a macOS menu bar app that lets you control your Yoto players. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Collect

Yo Remote does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. We operate no backend infrastructure.

Yoto Account Authentication

Yo Remote uses OAuth 2.0 to authenticate with your Yoto account. Your Yoto credentials are entered directly on Yoto's website and are never seen or stored by Yo Remote. The resulting access token is stored securely in your Mac's system Keychain and is used solely to communicate with the Yoto API on your behalf.

Data Stored Locally

  • Keychain: Your Yoto OAuth access and refresh tokens.
  • UserDefaults: App preferences such as your default player selection and menu bar display options.

None of this data leaves your device except as required to communicate with Yoto's own servers.

Third-Party Services

Yo Remote communicates exclusively with Yoto's official API and MQTT infrastructure to retrieve player status and send playback commands. We are not affiliated with Yoto Limited. Please refer to Yoto's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle your data.

Analytics & Tracking

Yo Remote contains no analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind.

Children's Privacy

Yo Remote does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the effective date above.
